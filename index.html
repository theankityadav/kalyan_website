App Features

Are you a fan of games that combine skill, strategy, and a touch of luck? Look no further! Kalyan Bazar present to you an exhilarating collection of seven different types of games that will keep you entertained and engaged for hours on end. From single-digit to full sangam, each game offers a unique experience. So, sign up now to play and experience fast results that will get your heart racing!

Single Digit:


  • Simple and straightforward gameplay.
  • Players choose a single number between 0 and 9.
  • Fast results, as the winning number is drawn quickly.
  • High potential for quick wins and instant gratification.

Jodi Digit:


  • Combines the excitement of single-digit selection with pairing numbers.
  • Players choose two numbers between 00 and 99 to form a "jodi" (pair).
  • Offers the possibility of doubling the chances of winning.
  • Engaging for players who enjoy strategizing their number combinations.

Single Pana:


  • Players select a three-digit number from 000 to 999.
  • Requires precision and intuition as players aim to match the winning combination.
  • Potential for significant rewards when the selected number matches the results.
  • Provides a variety of number combinations to choose from.

Double Pana:


  • Builds on the single pana concept by requiring three-digit numbers with repeated digits.
  • Players select numbers like 112, 323, or 909.
  • Multiplies the excitement and potential rewards compared to single pana.
  • Appeals to players who prefer higher stakes and more challenging gameplay.

Triple Pana:


  • Takes the stakes even higher with three-digit numbers consisting of the same digit repeated three times (e.g., 777 or 444).
  • Offers substantial rewards for successful matches.
  • Intense and adrenaline-pumping gameplay for thrill-seekers.

Half Sangam:


  • A combination of single-digit and jodi digit games.
  • Players choose a single-digit number and pair it with a two-digit number.
  • Enhances the chances of winning by having multiple possibilities for matching results.
  • Keeps players engaged with versatile gameplay options.

Full Sangam:


  • The ultimate challenge, combining single-digit and three-digit number selection.
  • Players select a single-digit number and pair it with a three-digit number.
  • Offers the most significant potential for rewards, but requires both parts of the combination to match the results.
  • Appeals to players who enjoy high-risk, high-reward gameplay.
